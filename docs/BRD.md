# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project Name
Chennai Data/Business Analyst Job Market Intelligence Dashboard

## Business Problem
Fresher job seekers targeting Data Analyst, Business Analyst, and BI Analyst roles in Chennai lack visibility into what skills, tools, and experience levels employers are actually asking for right now. Job seekers rely on generic advice instead of current, local market data — leading to misdirected upskilling and application effort.

## Goal
Build a data-driven view of the Chennai analyst job market that answers: which skills are most in demand, how experience requirements vary by role title, which companies are actively hiring, and how these patterns shift over time.

## Target User
Job seekers (fresher analysts), and secondarily, students/career counselors advising them.

## Success Criteria
- A working dataset of 150+ Chennai analyst job postings
- A dashboard showing skill frequency, experience-level distribution, and hiring company trends
- Insights that are actually usable — e.g., "SQL appears in 80% of postings, Data Studio in only 10%"

## Scope (v1)
- Roles: Data Analyst, Business Analyst, BI Analyst, Junior Data Analyst
- Location: Chennai only
- Data source: Indeed job listings
- Refresh: manual/one-time pull for v1 (not real-time)

## Out of Scope (v1)
- Salary analysis (data mostly unavailable on Indeed listings)
- Multi-city comparison
- Real-time auto-refresh
- LinkedIn data
